About Us

Marine Robotics Society is a student-led group at University of Strathclyde, fostering innovation in marine autonomous systems.

Student-Led Innovation

Organised by students, for students, we provide hands-on experience with cutting-edge marine robotics technology.

Technical Projects

Work on real-world autonomous systems, developing technical, soft, and leadership skills through practical experience.

Regular Activities

Join our weekly meetings every Wednesday at 5 PM, plus workshops and presentations throughout the year.

Industry Connections

Collaborate with industry partners to enhance student experience and build professional networks.

Team StrathVoyager

Our competitive team focusing on the development of StrathVoyager Autonomous Surface Vehicles, participating in international marine robotics competitions.
