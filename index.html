About us

Marine Robotics Society is a student-led group at University of Strathclyde, which provides a space for all enthusiasts of robotics technologies in the marine environment by organising technical projects and research opportunities. Organised from students, for students, the society provides the opportunity to students to work on their technical, soft and leadership skills and gain practical experience working with latest autonomy technology. Next to weekly meetings (Wednesdays, 5 pm) the society organises workshops and presentations and offers collaboration with businesses to enhance student experience and connect businesses with student talent.
